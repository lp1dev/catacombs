C A T A C O M B S


A sound-based exploration game

Game Rules


You are lost in the catacombs, your flashlight just died and you don't have any other light source around you.


Your goal is to successfully get out without dying. You have 3 lives which will be used if you walk on glass or are bitten by rats.


Each map is randomly generated and have at least one exit


Important: This game is supposed to be played with a headset, sounds enabled, a pen, a paper and without using the minimap which is only here if you want to see what the map looks like after playing it.
